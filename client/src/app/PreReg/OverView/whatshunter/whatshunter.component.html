The Traveling Salesman problem is an example. The salesperson is to visit each of 15 cities and stop at each only once; find the shortest route. Using traditional searching computing methods, this involves about 1.6 Trillion combinations taking centuries to solve.

Historically, the approach to solve harder problems had been to use computers working together for days, weeks, or or longer. Not only expensive, it often didn't produce results in the time needed.

Hunter has been designed with advanced computing methods along with leading-edge enhancements to "Genetic Algorithms" and gets that answer much sooner.
